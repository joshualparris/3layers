# Vision And Product Pillars

## Product Vision

`Epic-Quest-Saga` is a narrative continuity operating app for tabletop campaigns: a hybrid of RPG companion, canon ledger, and session command center.

It should feel as dependable for campaign memory as Notion is for team knowledge, while preserving the emotional stakes and narrative integrity expected from a premium RPG experience.

## Product Outcomes

- Zero-loss continuity across sessions.
- Fast decision support during live play.
- Clear separation between source canon, table canon, and unresolved facts.
- Respectful handling of player agency and safety constraints.
- Seamless progression from campaign record to tactical execution.

## Core Pillars

1. Canon Integrity
   - Every fact has status and provenance.
   - Contradictions are surfaced, not hidden.
2. Session Utility
   - Key information is retrievable in under 10 seconds during play.
   - High-signal dashboards over raw text dumps.
3. Human-Centered Narrative
   - Emotional and relationship threads are first-class entities, not side notes.
   - Social and safety constraints are enforceable, not optional.
4. Upgradeability
   - New source docs can be ingested without re-architecting the app.
   - Data model remains explicit and strongly typed.
5. Indie AAA Discipline
   - Product management, QA, release, and risk practices are documented and repeatable.

## Audience

- Primary: table owner / campaign coordinator (Josh).
- Secondary: DM and players consuming approved continuity state.
- Tertiary: future collaborators maintaining code/data/ops.

## Scope Boundaries

- In scope: continuity, campaign operations, character and quest state, tactical prep.
- Out of scope (current phase): multiplayer sync backend, account system, monetization, live cloud services.
